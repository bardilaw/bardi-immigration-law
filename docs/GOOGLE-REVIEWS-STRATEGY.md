# Bardi Immigration Law — First 5 Google Reviews Strategy

> **Owner:** CMO (BAR-48) | **Last updated:** 2026-05-19
> **Roadmap item:** 3.9 — Initial 5 Google reviews strategy
> **Goal:** 5 verified Google reviews at or before June 10, 2026 launch

---

## Why 5 Reviews Matter

Google Business Profiles don't display a star rating until a listing has at least 5 reviews. Without 5, the profile shows "No reviews" — a credibility gap that competitors with 100+ reviews will exploit.

**5 reviews at launch = visible star rating = credibility signal for new visitors.**

Getting to 5 is the most important review milestone. After that, velocity matters more than volume.

---

## Legal/Ethical Constraints

Bar rules (Georgia State Bar, Rule 7.3; Alabama Rules of Professional Conduct 7.3) prohibit:
- Soliciting reviews in exchange for anything of value
- Soliciting reviews from current clients in active matters
- Fake or manufactured reviews

**Safe to ask:**
- Past clients whose matters are fully concluded
- Colleagues / co-counsel who have worked with Attorney Bardi professionally
- Professional contacts who can attest to competency (peers, referral partners)
- Non-client professional references (law school professors, AILA colleagues)

**Do not ask:**
- Current clients with open matters
- Anyone in exchange for any benefit

## Review Quality Guidance (Optional — share with reviewers if they ask)

If a reviewer asks "what should I say?" — suggest they address one or more of:

- How the case was handled (communication, attention to detail, outcomes)
- What it was like to work directly with the attorney (vs. being handed off)
- Whether they would recommend Bardi Immigration Law to a friend or family member
- Specific practice area if comfortable mentioning it

**Do not:** provide specific language to copy. Reviewers should use their own words.

---

## After Launch — Review Velocity Plan

5 reviews at launch is the floor. Sustain momentum with:

1. **Post-matter review request** — send the review link to every closed case (past clients only)
2. **Referral partner asks** — attorneys who refer cases can leave professional reviews
3. **Response protocol** — respond to every review (positive and negative) within 48 hours; shows engagement and improves local SEO

**Target:** 15 reviews within 90 days of launch; 30 within 6 months.
