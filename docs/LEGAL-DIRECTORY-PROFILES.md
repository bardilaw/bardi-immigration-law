## Platform-by-Platform Setup Instructions

### 1. Avvo (avvo.com)

**Profile type:** Free basic listing (auto-generated from bar records); claim and enhance it.
**Priority:** HIGH — Avvo is heavily indexed by Google for "[practice area] attorney [city]" searches.

**Steps:**
1. Go to avvo.com → click "Are you a lawyer?" (top nav)
2. Search your name + state to find the auto-generated listing
3. Click "Claim my profile" → verify via bar number + email
4. Complete the profile:
   - Upload a professional headshot (square crop, 400×400px min)
   - Paste the **Short Bio** above
   - Add all practice areas from the list above
   - Add bar admissions with exact dates
   - Add AILA membership
   - Add languages
   - Add website: https://bardilaw.com
5. **Request reviews:** Avvo's review system requires clients to rate you. After go-live, send the Avvo profile link to satisfied clients and ask for a review. Target: 5+ reviews in first 90 days.

**Avvo Rating note:** Avvo auto-calculates a numeric rating (1–10) from bar records, peer endorsements, and client reviews. A fresh profile typically starts at 6–7. Add endorsements from attorney colleagues to push toward 8+.

---

### 2. Martindale-Hubbell (martindale.com)

**Profile type:** Free basic listing; AV Preeminent rating is earned (peer review based).
**Priority:** HIGH — Martindale carries significant credibility signals with referring attorneys and Google.

**Steps:**
1. Go to martindale.com → "Claim Your Profile" (lawyers.com and martindale.com share profiles via LexisNexis)
2. Search for existing listing; if none, create at lawyers.com (same network)
3. Claim via bar verification
4. Complete the profile:
   - Upload professional headshot
   - Paste the **Long Bio** above
   - Add all practice areas
   - Add bar admissions
   - Add AILA affiliation
   - Add languages
   - Add website
5. **AV Preeminent rating:** Not required at launch; this is a peer-review process that takes time. Worth pursuing in Year 2 as a credibility signal.

---

### 3. FindLaw (findlaw.com)

**Profile type:** Free basic attorney directory listing.
**Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH — FindLaw ranks well in general legal searches; less SEO-dominant than Avvo but trusted by older demographics.

**Steps:**
1. Go to lawyers.findlaw.com → "Are you an attorney?" → "Claim or Create a Profile"
2. Search for existing listing by name + state
3. Verify via email and bar number
4. Complete the profile:
   - Upload headshot
   - Paste the **Long Bio** (FindLaw allows more text than Avvo)
   - Add all practice areas — select Immigration as primary, add specific sub-areas
   - Add bar info, languages, firm details
   - Website: https://bardilaw.com
5. FindLaw also offers paid "sponsored attorney" upgrades — skip at launch, revisit after 6 months if SEO organic traffic is insufficient.

---

### 4. Justia (justia.com)

**Profile type:** Free lawyer directory listing; also indexes case law, which links back to attorney profiles.
**Priority:** MEDIUM — strong domain authority; free profiles are high-value for the cost.

**Steps:**
1. Go to lawyers.justia.com → "Create a Lawyer Profile" or search for an existing listing
2. Register with your bar email
3. Complete the profile:
   - Upload headshot
   - Paste the **Short Bio** (Justia's bio field is more limited in displayed length)
   - Select practice areas — Immigration is a top-level category; add sub-areas in the notes/extended fields
   - Add bar admissions, languages, AILA membership
   - Website: https://bardilaw.com
4. Justia also allows linking to published legal articles — once the Bardi blog is live, add article links here.

## Photo Guidelines (applies to all platforms)

- **Format:** JPG or PNG, square crop preferred (platforms will crop non-square images)
- **Min size:** 400×400px; 800×800px preferred for high-DPI displays
- **Background:** Solid or simple (light gray, white, or professional office). No busy backgrounds.
- **Attire:** Professional — business formal or business professional
- **Expression:** Warm, approachable, confident — this is a trust signal, not a passport photo
- **Brand note:** Align with BRAND-IDENTITY.md — boutique, minimalist, trustworthy. Same photo across all platforms for consistency.
