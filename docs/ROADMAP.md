# Bardi Immigration Law — Roadmap

**Company Goal:** Launch an independent immigration law firm with a professional brand, online presence, and the systems needed to attract and convert clients.

**Success State:** Firm is findable online, has a credible brand, a functioning bilingual website with lead capture, and initial client pipeline in motion.

**Launch Deadline:** June 10, 2026 (3 weeks from project start)

> This roadmap is compressed from the original 10-week plan to meet the client's June 10 launch target. See `CLIENT-BRIEF.md` for full client intake data.

---
