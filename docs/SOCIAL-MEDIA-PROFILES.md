## Platform-by-Platform Setup Instructions

---

### INSTAGRAM

**Account type:** Professional — Business (not Creator)

**Setup steps:**
1. Go to instagram.com → Create account → Use business email (info@bardilaw.com)
2. Username: `bardiimmigrationlaw` (or fallback)
3. Switch to Professional Account: Profile → Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account → Business
4. Category: **Lawyer & Law Firm**
5. Upload profile photo (round white-circle logo, 400×400px min)
6. **Bio (150 char max):**
   ```
   Immigration attorney serving GA & AL. Boutique practice. Direct attorney access. English | Español | Magyar.
   ```
7. Website: `https://bardilaw.com`
8. Contact button: email → `info@bardilaw.com`; phone → `[ATTORNEY TO SUPPLY]`
9. **Connect to Facebook Business Page** (created in step below) via Meta Business Suite — this links the accounts for unified management

**Optional at launch:**
- Create a highlight cover for "About Us" and "Services" (can be done after launch)
- Pin a welcome reel once content begins

---

### FACEBOOK

**Account type:** Facebook Business Page (not personal profile)

**Setup steps:**
1. Log into Facebook with attorney personal account (required to create a Business Page)
2. Go to facebook.com/pages/create
3. Page name: **Bardi Immigration Law**
4. Category: **Lawyer & Law Firm**
5. Username/handle: `bardiimmigrationlaw` (set via Page Settings → Page Info)
6. Upload profile photo (round white-circle logo)
7. Upload cover photo — recommend a simple branded banner (Navy `#1B2A4A` background + "Bardi Immigration Law" in white, or a professional photo of the attorney). Dimensions: 820×312px
8. **About section:**
   - Short description (255 chars):
     ```
     Boutique immigration law firm serving GA & AL. Direct attorney access. DACA · Removal Defense · Family Immigration · Federal Litigation.
     ```
   - Website: `https://bardilaw.com`
   - Email: `info@bardilaw.com`
   - Phone: `[ATTORNEY TO SUPPLY]`
   - Long description: use the Extended version bio above
9. Services section: Add immigration law services (DACA, Green Cards, Removal Defense, etc.)
10. Book Now button (optional): Link to bardilaw.com/contact once site is live
11. **Connect to Instagram** via Meta Business Suite (instagram.com → Professional Dashboard → Connect accounts)

**Meta Business Suite:**
After both accounts exist, go to business.facebook.com to manage both from one dashboard.

**Setup steps:**

3. Username: `bardiimmigrationlaw`

6. Upload profile photo (round white-circle logo)
7. **Bio (80 char max):**
