# Bardi Immigration Law — Vision

## Vision Statement

Become the immigration law practice that individuals and families in the Southeast United States trust first — known for boutique care, creative legal strategy, and direct attorney communication.

## Mission

Deliver expert, bilingual immigration legal representation with the personal attention of a boutique practice and the competence of a seasoned litigator. Make the immigration process navigable so clients can focus on building their lives in the United States.

## Strategic Milestones

### Milestone 1: Foundation (May 19 – May 25, 2026)

Establish the firm's identity, brand, and digital infrastructure based on client intake data.

- Client brief synthesized and distributed to all agents
- Brand identity refined: boutique, minimalist, trustworthy; light backgrounds; round logo for social
- Market analysis and competitive landscape updated with actual practice areas and named competitors
- Tech stack decided
- Repo initialized

**Exit criteria:** CLIENT-BRIEF.md approved, all strategy docs updated, tech stack locked.

### Milestone 2: Website MVP (May 26 – June 5, 2026)

Ship a professional, bilingual, SEO-ready website with consultation booking.

- Practice area pages covering benefits (DACA, SIJS, VAWA, U-visa, TPS, family-based, waivers), removal defense, and federal litigation
- English + Spanish translation
- Consultation booking flow (form-based at minimum; direct booking TBD)
- On-page SEO, analytics, and lead capture

**Exit criteria:** Website deployed, all pages live in EN+ES, consultation form working, Lighthouse >= 85.

### Milestone 3: Online Presence & Launch (June 5 – June 10, 2026)

Go live across digital channels where clients actually are.

- Google Business Profile verified
- Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube profiles created
- Legal directory profiles (Avvo, Justia, FindLaw)
- Domain email and email signature

**Exit criteria:** Website at final domain, Google profile live, social profiles active, directory listings published.

### Milestone 4: Credibility and Traction (June – December 2026)

Build social proof and a repeatable client pipeline.

- Review generation system (Google reviews)
- Content cadence on Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube
- Community visibility (AILA chapter connections, immigrant-serving organizations)
- Referral outreach to feeder attorneys (family law, criminal defense)
- Track conversion: inquiry -> consultation -> retained, by source
- Evaluate personal injury practice area expansion based on demand

**Exit criteria:** 10+ Google reviews at 4.5+ stars, steady monthly inquiry volume, break-even on operating costs.

## Non-Goals

- **Multi-attorney expansion in Year 1.** Build a sustainable solo practice first.
- **Factory-style intake.** Client interaction starts with an attorney, not an intake specialist. This is a core differentiator.
- **Competing on price.** Compete on care, creative strategy, and direct communication.
- **Custom-built technology.** Use proven off-the-shelf tools for practice management, billing, and CRM.
- **LinkedIn-first marketing.** Client's audience is on Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Google Search — not LinkedIn.
- **Dark/flashy website aesthetic.** Client prefers light backgrounds, minimalist design. Not Antonini & Cohen level of visual complexity.
